Channel appÔÉÅ

Channels can optionally have an app. The app describes the rules for updating the channel state and for interpreting the channel data. No app is required for payment channels.

An app consists of two parts:

  1. App backed in the perun client: To validate each off-chain transaction during the transact phase.

  2. App smart contract on the blockchain: To validate each force-update transaction during the finalize phase.

To use an app in a channel,

  1. The app smart contract corresponding to the app must be deployed on the blockchain and referenced at the time of channel proposal and,

  2. The app backend must be supported by the perun clients of all the channel participants.

In the next section, protocols for each of the four phases in the life cycle of a state channel are described.

Note

All channel types can have an app. However, the current implementation of go-perun supports using apps only in ledger channels and sub-channels.
